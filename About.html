About Us

Berimbolo-Security

Berimbolo-Securityis a website for a protection and insurance company that helps provide people with security information and solutions to problems that may have a security impact. The company offers some services that help the user in the protection process and sells devices used in monitoring and protection. Berimbolo Security subject to the provisions of Law 159 of 1981, established with a licensed capital of two million five hundred thousand dolar, and it is one of the companies specialized in the work of guarding and securing facilities from within. And this is in accordance with Law No. 86 of 2015 and Ministerial Resolution No. 133 of 2016, which is authorized to practice security and guarding from the Ministry of Interior and all concerned authorities .
